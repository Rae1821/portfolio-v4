About

Hello again! I'm a solution-oriented frontend web developer based out of Chattanooga, TN. I am passionate about bringing digital products to life and creating online experiences that are delightful to use. I am constantly building and learning new technologies to continue to grow my skills in web development.

I spent 10+ years fine-tuning my communication and leadership skills with roles in customer service and higher education. I started my coding journey after being laid off unexpectedly. I then decided to take a career break to raise my son. Once he went to kindergarten I knew I was ready to pursue coding full time and I am loving every second of it! In my spare time I design + create custom garments and play superheros with my son.
